CS-16-LM-200

Compact Series

Product Description

Compact series machines are 2-axes horizontal turning centers with a blend of precision & compact footprint. This model is capable of machining components up to Dia 330 mm and 200 mm in length, offered with Servo Turret, and comes with either Integral Spindle or External Motor Mounted with a Belt Driven spindle.
